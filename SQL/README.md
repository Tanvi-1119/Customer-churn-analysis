**Customer Churn Analysis Using SQL**

**Project Overview**
This project analyzes customer churn using SQL. The goal is to identify patterns in customer behavior, contract types, demographics, and internet services that may influence customer churn.

**SQL Concepts Used**
SELECT
WHERE
ORDER BY
GROUP BY
COUNT()
AVG()
SUM()
CASE WHEN
DISTINCT

**Analysis Performed**

**Customer Overview**
Total customers
Average monthly charges
Average tenure
Total revenue

**Churn Analysis**
Churn distribution
Churn by contract type
Churn by payment method

**Contract Analysis**
Customer count by contract
Average monthly charges by contract

**Demographic Analysis**
Gender distribution
Senior citizen analysis
Partner and dependent analysis

**Internet Service Analysis**
Customer distribution by internet service
Churn by internet service
Additional service analysis

**Key Findings**
Month-to-month contracts had the highest churn.
Customers using Fiber Optic internet showed higher churn than DSL customers.
Customers with longer contracts were more likely to stay.
The overall churn rate was approximately 26.5%.
Monthly charges varied across customer segments and contract types.
