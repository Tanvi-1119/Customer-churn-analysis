# IBM Telco Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn in the IBM Telco dataset to identify the key factors contributing to customer attrition and recommend business strategies to improve customer retention. The analysis was conducted using SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

## Business Problem

IBM Telecom is experiencing a 26% customer churn rate, resulting in the loss of recurring revenue and customer retention opportunities. The objective of this project is to identify the main drivers of churn and recommend practical business actions to reduce customer attrition.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns across different customer segments.
* Identify the factors contributing to high churn rates.
* Validate findings using SQL, Excel, and Power BI.
* Recommend business strategies to improve customer retention.

## Tools and Technologies Used

* **SQL** – Data querying and analysis
* **Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation

## Key Findings

* The overall customer churn rate is **26%**.
* **23.5%** of the total customer base consists of churned month-to-month contract customers.
* **18.42%** of the total customer base consists of churned fiber optic service users.
* **6.76%** of the total customer base consists of churned senior citizen customers.
* Service dissatisfaction and inadequate product guidance appear to be major contributors to customer churn.

## Business Recommendations

* Improve customer service and product guidance channels.
* Enhance onboarding support for month-to-month customers.
* Offer retention incentives to convert satisfied month-to-month customers into longer-term contracts.
* Increase billing transparency by providing a detailed charge breakdown in the customer portal.
* Investigate the reasons behind fiber optic customer dissatisfaction and churn.

## Success Metrics

* Reduce the overall customer churn rate.
* Increase retention among month-to-month customers.
* Improve customer satisfaction with fiber optic services.
* Reduce customer complaints related to billing and product guidance.
